Rationale

My name is Casey Kinsey. I'm a software engineering consultant from Northwest Arkansas. I love projects, and I love solving problems. Check out my website, and my company.

I type for a living, and so I frequently take typing tests to warm myself up before a day's work to prevent fatigue and injury. There's a pretty ubiquitous website out there for typing tests, and I won't point any fingers, but it sucks. Really badly. So I decided for a weekend project, I'd make my own.

Texts are from the public domain and provided courtesy of the very cool folks at textbookrevolution.org.

Total development time so far: 7 hours
