Rationale

My name is Casey Kinsey, and I'm a software engineering consultant hailing from Northwest Arkansas. I love projects and solving problems. Check out my website, and my company.

I type for a living, and so I frequently take typing tests to warm myself up before a day's work to prevent fatigue and injury. There's a pretty ubiquitous website out there for typing tests, and though I won't point any specific fingers--it sucks. Really badly. So I decided for a weekend project, I'd make my own.

Texts are from the public domain and provided courtesy of the very cool folks at textbookrevolution.org.

Feel free to judge my source code mercilessly on GitHub

Total development time so far: 9 hours
